Rules

Welcome to Small Draw! This is a game where you need to explain words with simplistic doodles.

Number of players: 2

Playing time: 2-3 min/round

To play a game, you need to host it first (click on the "Host tab"). Choose the number of rounds and click "Create". You'll be given an id which you and your partner will need to enter on the start page. Choose whether you want to play first or second (don't choose the same as your partner, it'll probably break the game.)

The first player then chooses a word category: Normal, Fake or Hard. Then they choose one of 4 words randomly selected from our dictionary.

Depending on the gamemode, they need to doodle a word in some sort of a "minimal" way (for now, there's just one gamemode where you need to draw as quick as possible).

This picture is sent to the second player that has to guess what word their opponent meant. If they guess correctly, they get 100 points, and their opponent receives 50-200 points depending on their efficiency (hard words increase the points by 50%, fake words double them.) Then, players switch their roles.

After several rounds, the player who has more points wins.
